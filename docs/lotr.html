Lord of the Rings

The following visualisation demonstrates pathpy's temporal visualisation features. The animation shows the temporal network of character co-ocurrences in sentences of the three novels of The Lord of The Rings, i.e. a link between two characters at time i indicates that those two characters were both mentioned in the i-th sentence of the novel. For convenience, the animation shows the book and chapter of the current sentence. Colors of nodes represent different factions, e.g. hobbits (green), elves (purple), fellowship (red), etc. Hovering over nodes will show the character name and the faction.

This example highlights pathpy's' advanced visualisation features, making use of custom templates and temporal network styling through Cascading Style Sheets.

Machine Learning for Complex Networks, CAIDAS, Julius-Maximilians-Universität Würzburg, 2022
